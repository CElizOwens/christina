About

Opus Oakland was created in October of 2017 by members of Oakland Civic Orchestra, a community orchestra affliated with Oakland Parks and Recreation.


The goal of Opus is to provide a regular small ensemble performance opportunity where interested musicians can meet other chamber music enthusiasts and expand their repertoire. As a result, cafe-goers around Oakland get the benefit of receiving free mini-concerts of an ever-changing variety of classical genres with fellow friends and music lovers in a casual, intimate atmosphere.
