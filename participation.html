Participation

Interested in performing?

We encourage interested musicians to attend one of our free events and peruse the recordings here on our site for reference and insight into our programming.

Please feel free to introduce yourself if you have interest in performing with us. We strive to bring together musicians of equal interest and enthusiasm to collaborate on suggested works and in various configurations of instrumentation.


We ask that individuals, only, perform with us upon first playing an Opus Oakland event; however, we are open to welcoming complete ensembles with moderate performance experience to perform selected works from their own repertoire (to be determined on a group-by-group basis).
