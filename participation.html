Participation

Interested in performing?

We encourage interested musicians to attend one of our free events and peruse the recordings here on our site for reference and insight into our vibe. Contact us at opusoakland@gmail.com with your instrument(s) and any pieces (as well as the part you would like to play) that you have interest in performing. As a collective, we have the luxury of being able to bring together musicians of equal interest and enthusiasm for the various works and configurations of instrumentation that are proposed.


We ask that individuals, only, perform with us upon first playing an Opus Oakland event; however, we are open to welcoming complete ensembles with moderate performance experience to perform selected works from their own repertoire (to be determined on a group-by-group basis).
